### **4. Hero Ingredients & Why**

- **Ceramide NP + Ceramide NS**: Dual ceramide complex that mirrors the skin's natural lipid structure; strengthens the ultra-thin under-eye barrier
- **Vaccinium Angustifolium (Blueberry) Extract**: Rich in anthocyanins — powerful antioxidants that protect against environmental aging and support microcirculation
- **Spirulina Platensis Extract**: Algae-derived nourishment with vitamins, minerals, and amino acids; supports skin resilience
- **6-Type Hyaluronic Acid Complex**: Sodium Hyaluronate, Hydroxypropyltrimonium HA, Sodium Acetylated HA, Hydrolyzed HA, Sodium Hyaluronate Crosspolymer, Potassium Hyaluronate — each with different molecular weights for multi-layer hydration
- **Hydrolyzed Extensin**: Plant-derived protein that supports skin structure and elasticity
- **Allantoin**: Gentle soothing agent that calms and conditions delicate skin

### **5. How to Use**

- **PM Routine preferred** (also suitable for AM)
- Apply to clean, dry under-eye area
- Leave on for minimum 10–15 minutes (can be left longer or overnight)
- Remove and gently pat remaining essence into skin
- Follow with eye cream if desired (Argireline™ Eye Cream)
- **60 patches per jar** = 30 uses (approximately 1 month supply)
